Contact Us

For all inquiries, please contact nohandsbrass@gmail.com.

If requesting a performance, kindly include the following information:

  • Location
  • Proposed rate
  • Total playing time, as well as span of time between first and last song (if different)
  • Preference for 5-piece or 8-piece ensemble
    • Our 5-piece ensemble is great for smaller spaces and the traditional Dixieland sound. Our 8-piece ensemble is our full strength and allows us to perform a wider range of creative arrangements and original tunes.
  • Any other special requests or pertinent information
